Aurora (born February 24, 2017) is an EASA engineer and astronaut.

Background

Aurora was born on February 24, 2017, in Bulsan, a mountainous region in Kirea known for its stunning natural landscapes. Shy and introspective, Aurora preferred quiet pursuits, developing an early interest in technology and complex systems. She studied at the University of Kirea, graduating with honors in Engineering Sciences, focusing on spacecraft systems.

Stardust 21

Aurora joined the KIRA Astronaut Training Program in 2039, quickly distinguishing herself in spacecraft maintenance and repair. Selected for the Stardust 21 mission in collaboration with EASA, she serves as the mission’s engineer, where she will conduct essential repairs and updates to the MIDST telescope’s systems. Despite her aversion to the spotlight, Aurora’s skills make her an indispensable part of the team.

Personal life

Aurora has maintained a lifelong friendship with fellow astronaut Concorde, whom she met during childhood. Outside her engineering career, Aurora is an avid competitive video gamer, known for her exceptional skills in multiplayer strategy games. She is deeply private and avoids photographs whenever possible, preferring to let her technical expertise speak for itself.
